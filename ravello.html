Ravello

is a town and comune situated above the Amalfi Coast in the province of Salerno, Campania, southern Italy, with approximately 2,500 inhabitants. Its scenic location makes it a popular tourist destination, and earned it a listing as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1996. (Source)

I visited Ravello this past summer with my boyfriend. It was super relaxing and a great way to end our trip.

We visited Villa Rufolo, a gorgeous villa with beautiful architecture and gardens.
